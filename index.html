Introduction

I received my PhD degree from Department of Computer Science and Engineering, The Hong Kong University of Science and Technology, supervised by Prof. Long Quan. Before that, I received my bachelor degree from School of Electronic Science and Engineering, Nanjing University in 2015.

My research interests are in the area of 3D computer vision, including multi-view stereo (MVS) for 3D reconstruction and deep learning with geometry. I have developed an accurate, large-scale MVS pipeline, which have achieved the state-of-the-art results on the Tanks and Temple benchmark, and have been integrated into the world's largest 3D reconstruction platform Altizure. I am currently working on MVSNet and BlendedMVS projects, and I am excited about researches which integrate traditional multi-view geometries with the deep learning techniques.
