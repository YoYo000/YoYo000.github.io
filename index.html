Introduction

I am a senior researcher at Apple. I received my PhD degree from CSE, HKUST in 2019 (supervisor: Prof. Long Quan), and bachelor degree from ECE, NJU in 2015 (advisor: Prof. Xun Cao). I was also a founding member of and a research scientist at Altizure before joining Apple.

My research interests lie in intersections of computer vision and computer graphics, including 3D reconstruction and differentiable rendering. I have developed the MVSNet series of networks for multi-view stereo reconstruction, and have built the large-scale BlendedMVS dataset for geometry related learning tasks. Currently I am working on differentiable rendering, including neural surface reconstruction, material estimation, and their applications to 3D photogrammetry pipelines.
